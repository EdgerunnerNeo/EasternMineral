About Eastern Mineral

Mine-to-Market.
Built for Consistency.

Eastern Mineral is a vertically integrated critical metals company. By controlling our own mining operations in Cambodia, we deliver supply certainty that commodity traders cannot — from extraction through export to final delivery across Asia-Pacific, the Middle East, and beyond.

With offices in Cambodia, Malaysia, and Dubai, we combine on-the-ground operational depth with institutional-grade trading capabilities, serving procurement teams and industrial buyers who require reliable, compliant, long-term supply.

How We Trade

From Sourcing
to Settlement

Our trading process is built for institutional counterparties — structured, transparent, and designed to close efficiently with full documentation at every stage.

01
Inquiry & Requirements
Submit a trade inquiry specifying metal category, grade, volume, and delivery destination. Our team responds with an initial term sheet within 48 hours.
02
KYC & Compliance Review
Both parties complete onboarding documentation including AML/KYC verification, corporate registry, and beneficial ownership disclosure.
03
Contract & Trade Financing
Formal sale-purchase agreement executed. Payment terms structured via letter of credit (LC), documentary collection, or pre-agreed wire transfer.
04
Logistics & Documentation
Material shipped with full documentation: certificate of analysis (CoA), bill of lading, packing list, customs clearance support, and insurance certificates.
05
Settlement & Post-Trade
Final settlement upon delivery confirmation. Ongoing relationship management and market intelligence provided to active trading partners.
